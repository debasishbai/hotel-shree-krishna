~45 km · 1 hr drive

One of Odisha's most visited Shakti Peethas. As a top hotel near Maa Tarini Temple, we offer early breakfast for pilgrims.

Mythological origin of the River Brahmani — spiritually significant and scenically beautiful.

Tranquil Shiva shrine in the verdant hills of Thakurmunda, surrounded by natural beauty.
